01 ABOUT

More than just finding bugs.

I am a Quality Assurance Engineer with over 3 years of experience in software testing, covering both web and mobile applications.

After completing my studies in Information Systems, I started my professional career as a Business Analyst at PT Pegadaian and then grew into a QA Engineer at PT Steradian Data Optima, where I handled enterprise banking projects (BRI/QCash), CRM, employee management, and digital wallet applications.

Currently, I work at Avows Technologies Indonesia, assigned to Bank Raya (Squad Payment), responsible for ensuring the quality of digital payment features.

I believe that a QA is not just tasked with finding bugs, but also serves as a product quality guardian — understanding business needs, providing input to the development process, and ensuring the user experience remains optimal.

"Quality is not just about catching defects. It's about building confidence that the product works exactly as promised to the user."
